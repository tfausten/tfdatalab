About

This blog deals with intellectual property statistics and other data analyses in R. It was built using the blogdown package for R. The theme is the example theme from blogdown and was forked from @jrutheiser/hugo-lithium-theme and modified by Yihui Xie.

This project is a learning experience for me in making and maintaining a statical website, as well as R and statistical analyses. While I will try to present interesting analyses and code examples, the contents should be taken with a grain of salt, as I have little experience in the matters I deal with here.

The site is hosted by Netlify under a subdomain kindly provided by the Rbind community.
